About Us

PrakritiPramana is a website dedicated to exploring the profound connection between nature (Prakriti) and the wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita. We believe that understanding and respecting the natural world is an essential part of living a meaningful and balanced life, as echoed in the timeless teachings of the Gita.
